How are topics assigned?
Topics will be provided by the JAMGOD, our judge of the event, right before it's your turn. Get ready for some spontaneous fun!

1. When does registration for the JAM event end?
Registration for the JAM JAM JAM event ends on 24th.
Make sure to sign up before the deadline to secure your spot!

2. Where will the JAM event be held?
The event will take place at C3 New Seminar Hall, Block C3, on 24th September.
Get ready for a full day of high-energy competition in this thrilling venue!

3. What is the time limit for each participant?
Each participant will be allotted exactly 1 minute to speak on the given topic.

4. How does the points system work?
Participants earn 1 point for every second they speak uninterrupted.
A valid challenge earns the challenger 3 additional points,
but an unsuccessful challenge leads to a deduction of 3 points.

5. Can I interrupt other participants during their turn?
Yes, participants can interrupt if they catch any hesitation, repetition, or deviation.
If your challenge is valid, you get to continue speaking for the remaining time!

The Young Orators Club

The Young Orators Club empowers youth through
effective communication and public speaking skills, fostering a supportive environment
where members build confidence and practice oratory techniques.
Our goal is to inspire critical thinking and creativity,
preparing members for future opportunities.
Vision: We envision a generation of articulate and
confident leaders who can communicate their ideas passionately
and make a positive impact in their communities and beyond.
Through our programs, we aim to cultivate a culture of
empowered voices that inspire change and innovation.
