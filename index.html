FAQs

1. When does registration for the JAM event end?

Registration for the JAM JAM JAM event ends on 24th. Make sure to sign up before the deadline to secure your spot!

2. Where will the JAM event be held?

>

The event will take place at C3 New Seminar Hall, Block C3, on 24th September. Get ready for a full day of high-energy competition in this thrilling venue!

3. What is the time limit for each participant?

Each participant will be allotted exactly 1 minute to speak on the given topic.

4. How does the points system work?

Participants earn 1 point for every second they speak uninterrupted. A valid challenge earns the challenger 3 additional points. An unsuccessful challenge leads to a deduction of 3 points.

Can I interrupt other participants?

Yes, participants can interrupt if they catch any hesitation, repetition, or deviation. If your challenge is valid, you get to continue speaking for the remaining time!
